1.5 Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

Copyrights allows someone to creates an website and owns the rights to the image. Only the owner of the image has the rights to reproduce the work, display, copy and sell to other people
Many websites offers pictures or templates that are free or you can get permission from the original author to let them allow you to use the images or templates for your website.
If you want to publish information on your website from another site, you will be able to link to it or asked permission to publish it on your site.
