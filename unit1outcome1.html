1.4 - Describe the risks that might impact digital projects

The following issues can bring Risks to an ongoing project...

- Time Management (have you incorrectly scoped the project and promised to deliver in an impossible time frame? Have you accounted for unforseen circumstances that may hinder your project?)
- Resource (you may not have all the resources needed)
- Security (Viruses, Corruption of data, data loss)
- Network (outages to internet connection may occur)
- Malfunction (software or hardware may stop working)

If the above is addressed you will reduce risks to the livelyhood of your project!


1.5 - Describe how you would go about selecting and using IT systems and software

When selecting IT hardware and software the following should be considered...
- Scope of project (what is needed for the project and what budget you have to achieve the goal)
- Feature Comparison ( compare the different tools and software against your project and see which is more efficient for your budget. can you get it for free?)
- Practicality (Can you use the software? does the requirements meet your need?)

These are some of the things to consider when running a project.
